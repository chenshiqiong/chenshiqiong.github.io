两个列表转字典
list1 = ['key1','key2']
list2 = ['1','2']
dict(zip(list1,list2))
# {'key1':'1','key2':'2'}
嵌套列表转字典
list = [['k1','v1'],['k2','v2']]
dict(list)
# {'k1':'vi','k2':'v2'}
列表转字符串

字典转字符串

# "{'k1':'v1','k2':'v2'}"
字典key，value互转

{value:key for key,value in dict.items()}
# {'v1':'k1','v2':'k2'}
